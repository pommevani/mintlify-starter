## Whitelist

**WHY IS FLARE LEAVING? WHY IS HE SENDING A MESSAGE?**

> To make sure we know the server owners, you need to be whitelisted. But how to make it? To keep it simple, read this:

- Join our server in the navigation bar (Support).
- Open a ticket to get support.
- Send your request and why do you need flare.
- Get whitelisted and enjoy our app.
